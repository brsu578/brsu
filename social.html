    Serving our community through compassion, solidarity, and action

    Our Commitment to Community Service

    At BRSU, we are dedicated to supporting underserved communities through various aid initiatives. During the COVID-19 pandemic, we actively engaged in relief efforts to help those in need. Our ongoing activities include providing assistance to the poor and organizing blood donation drives to save lives and promote health within our community.

    Through our community service programs, we aim to improve living conditions, enhance well-being, and create opportunities for Rohingya refugees to support one another. Our approach emphasizes dignity, community participation, and sustainable solutions that empower rather than create dependency.

    BRSU's social service work is guided by the principle that those closest to the challenges often have the best insights into effective solutions. We mobilize Rohingya youth volunteers to lead service initiatives, working in partnership with community members, local organizations, and international humanitarian agencies.

    Our Service Initiatives

    Health & Wellness

    Our health initiatives include community health education, mental health support groups, and assistance accessing medical services. We train youth health advocates who provide information on disease prevention, nutrition, and hygiene practices.

    Child & Youth Support

    We provide safe spaces, recreational activities, and mentorship for children and youth. Our programs include sports clubs, arts activities, and peer support groups designed to promote resilience, social skills, and emotional well-being.

    Community Outreach

    Our outreach teams identify vulnerable individuals and families, connect them with resources, and provide direct assistance. We conduct regular community needs assessments to ensure our services address the most pressing concerns.

    Emergency Response

    Our emergency response team mobilizes quickly during crises such as fires, floods, and cyclones. We provide immediate assistance, coordinate with relief agencies, and help affected families access emergency services and supplies.

    Elder Care & Support

    We provide companionship, assistance, and advocacy for elderly community members. Our volunteers help with daily tasks, facilitate access to services, and organize social activities that honor the wisdom and contributions of our elders.

    Featured Programs

    BRSU Aid Program: Supporting Rohingya Families with Compassion and Care

    BRSU has actively organized and carried out aid distribution programs to support Rohingya families facing hardship. From delivering essential food packages and hygiene kits to distributing clothing and educational supplies, our goal is to ensure that no one in our community is left behind. These efforts are driven by our dedicated youth volunteers who work tirelessly on the ground, identifying urgent needs and delivering assistance with care, respect, and transparency. Through these initiatives, we aim to bring hope, relief, and dignity to those most in need.
