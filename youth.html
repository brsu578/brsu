    Youth Empowerment Program

    Shaping Tomorrow’s Leaders: Youth Empowerment in Action.

    Empowering Rohingya Youth

    The BRSU Youth Empowerment Program (YEP) is designed to equip young Rohingya with the skills, knowledge, and confidence they need to become effective leaders and change-makers in their communities. We believe that youth are not just the future—they are powerful agents of change today.

    Our program takes a holistic approach to youth development, addressing the unique challenges faced by Rohingya youth while building on their strengths, resilience, and potential. Through leadership training, skill development, mentorship, and hands-on project experience, we create pathways for youth to discover their voices and make meaningful contributions to community development.

    Program Components

    Leadership Development

    Our leadership development program builds essential skills for effective community leadership, including communication, problem-solving, conflict resolution, and ethical decision-making.

    • Youth Leadership Academy (8-week program)
    • Public Speaking Workshops
    • Community Organizing Training
    • Ethical Leadership Seminars

    Skills Building

    Our skills building workshops equip youth with practical skills that enhance their personal development, employability, and ability to contribute to community initiatives.

    • Digital Literacy & Technology Skills
    • Financial Literacy & Entrepreneurship
    • Creative Arts & Cultural Expression
    • Project Management & Organization

    Current Initiatives

    Youth Leadership Council

    A representative body of youth leaders who participate in decision-making, organize community events, and advocate for youth concerns. Council members are elected annually and receive intensive leadership training.

    Digital Ambassadors Program

    Training youth in digital skills and providing them with technology tools to become digital educators in their communities. Digital Ambassadors conduct workshops on computer literacy, online safety, and digital storytelling.

    Youth Peace Builders Network

    A network of youth trained in conflict resolution, mediation, and peace-building who work to promote harmony and understanding within and between communities.

    Environmental Action Team

    Youth environmentalists working on projects related to waste management, tree planting, and environmental education in the camps and surrounding areas.
