    Our Sports Initiative

    At BRSU, we believe that sports and recreational activities play a vital role in promoting physical health, mental well-being, and community cohesion. Our sports programs provide Rohingya youth with opportunities to develop teamwork, leadership, and resilience while enjoying the universal language of play.

    Through organized teams, regular training sessions, friendly matches, and tournaments, we create spaces where young people can exercise, socialize, and develop their talents. Our sports initiatives also serve as platforms for cultural exchange, conflict resolution, and community building.

    BRSU's sports programs are inclusive and accessible to all, regardless of skill level or prior experience. We particularly encourage the participation of girls and young women, who often have limited access to sports and recreational activities.

    Our Sports Programs

    Football is our most popular sport, with multiple teams across different age groups. Our football program includes regular training sessions, friendly matches with local teams, and participation in regional tournaments. The program focuses on developing technical skills, tactical understanding, teamwork, and sportsmanship.

    Volleyball

    Volleyball is a popular team sport in our community that requires minimal equipment and can be played in limited spaces. Our volleyball program teaches fundamental skills such as serving, passing, setting, and spiking, while promoting teamwork and communication. We have both competitive teams and recreational play options.

    Teams

    • BRSU Spikers (Mixed Team)
    • BRSU Women's Volleyball Team

    Indoor Games

    Indoor Ball Passing Program (Female)

    Our indoor ball passing program for girls and young women is growing steadily, highlighting the enthusiasm for team-based indoor games within our community. The program focuses on developing agility, coordination, and accurate passing skills through structured training sessions and friendly competitions. Alongside physical development, it encourages teamwork, communication, and leadership in a fun and inclusive environment. This initiative plays an important role in empowering female participants through active sports engagement.

    Teams

    • BRSU Storm Sisters (Senior Team)
    • BRSU Velocity Queens (Junior Team)

    Our Sports Facilities

    Main Football Field

    Our main football field is located in Block B of Kutupalong camp. The field features marked boundaries, goals, and a level playing surface maintained by our volunteer grounds team.

    Block B, Kutupalong Camp
    Open daily from 7 AM to 6 PM

    BRSU Recreation Center

    Our indoor recreation center houses facilities for volleyball, carrom, chess, ludo, and other indoor activities. The center provides a safe space for sports and games during monsoon season and extreme weather.

    BRSU Community Center, Block B
    Open daily from 9 AM to 8 PM
