Why Donate to BRSU?

Your generous donation to the Bangladesh Rohingya Student Union (BRSU) directly supports educational opportunities for Rohingya students. We use donations to provide scholarships, educational materials, mentorship programs, and community events that empower the next generation of Rohingya leaders.

Your Donation Supports:

  • Scholarships for promising students
  • Educational resources and study materials
  • Community workshops and skill development programs
  • Cultural preservation initiatives
  • Advocacy for educational rights
