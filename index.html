BRSU President Raises Rohingya Crisis at Senator Merkley’s Event; U.S. Senate Action Promised.

The president of BRSU was invited at an event organised by Senator Jeff Markely. There were invited from different communities in the event. I highlighted ongoing atrocities against Rohingya people including children and women in Rakhine. Mr Senator confirmed me he will bring the issues in the Senate. He will keep pushing the USA 🇺🇸 government to settle Rohingya issue permanently in rakhine...

BRSU Admin September, 2024

Coordination Meeting Held with CBO Representatives on September 1st

Agendas Discussed:

  • Rakhine situation
  • Bangladesh camps situation
  • Roles of CBOs in Rakhine and Bangladesh
  • Youth engagement
  • New arrivals
  • Opportunities for women

Everyone shared their thoughts based on the agendas. We are closely monitoring the situation in Rakhine and are working to collect accurate information and evidence. The meeting also emphasized gathering detailed data on new arrivals.

We shared updates on BRSU’s ongoing support during emergencies. Our efforts were acknowledged and appreciated by other CBOs for helping vulnerable people in critical situations.

Concerns were raised regarding the escalating violence in the camps. Nightly clashes between armed groups are creating fear and targeting community leaders, including human rights defenders and youth representatives.

The meeting also congratulated Dr. Younus on his appointment as Chief Advisor in Bangladesh’s current advisory panel. This was seen as a hopeful step toward more inclusive leadership.

All CBOs agreed to collaborate more closely for unified actions and stronger advocacy moving forward.

Our Mission

Our mission is to uplift Rohingya students and youth by expanding access to quality education, fostering leadership and civic engagement, promoting mental health and well-being, and preserving our cultural heritage. We are committed to grassroots organizing, global advocacy for a durable solution for genocide survivors, and community-driven action through meaningful partnerships—working to amplify Rohingya voices and create lasting impact for our people and future generations.
